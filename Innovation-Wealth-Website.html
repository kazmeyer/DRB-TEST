Banking site for 'wealth' clients

Specifically designed for clients looking to invest with the credit union, this site was a challenge to keep clean, simple, with some subtle touches. For example a full-featured hero ad for the home page with a myriad of options to allow the client to control every facet of their ad campaigns from colour choices down to animation.
