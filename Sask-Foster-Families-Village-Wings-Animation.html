A chance to flex some new muscles!

This project was an awesome team effort for a great client. My part: take the script and come up with a storyboard, create animatic from that, set it to music and line up the action with the voice over. Once the rough animatic was approved, I then took the photoshop files containing the characters and animated them in After Effects. The series was a chance for me to up my animation skills, but more importantly, proved to be a huge success for the client!
